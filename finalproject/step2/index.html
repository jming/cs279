Setup

You'll be presented a map with a route marked in blue and a streetview of your starting point on the map. Your goal is to travel from the red starting marker to the yellow end marker while marking the different obstacles.

Navigating

Pan around using the compass at the top left corner of the streetview or by dragging to spin the view.

Move by clicking on the arrows on the streetview or by clicking forward along the street.
Marking

For each road segment, mark if there are any obstacles in the road and which side of the road they occur on. Examples of obstacles are shown in the images below.
